Experimental & Characterization Techniques

State-of-the-art methodologies employed across synthesis, processing, and analysis

Solid-State Ceramic Synthesis

Conventional solid-state reaction route for bulk ceramic fabrication with controlled atmosphere sintering up to 1600 °C.

Sol-Gel Processing

Wet-chemical synthesis for nanoparticles, ceramics, and thin-film precursor preparation with compositional homogeneity at molecular level.

Pulsed Laser Deposition (PLD)

KrF excimer laser system for growth of high-quality epitaxial and polycrystalline oxide thin films on various substrates.

X-Ray Diffraction (XRD)

Structural characterization including phase analysis, lattice parameter determination, and Rietveld refinement using Bruker D8 Advance system.

Impedance Spectroscopy

Broad-band dielectric characterization from 10 Hz to 3 GHz for grain/grain-boundary analysis and relaxation studies.

Ferroelectric P-E Loop Tracer

Measurement of polarization-electric field hysteresis loops and electrostrain curves using a Radiant Precision Premier II system.

SEM / FESEM

High-resolution scanning electron microscopy for microstructural analysis and grain size determination; combined with EDX for elemental mapping.

FTIR & Raman Spectroscopy

Vibrational spectroscopy for bond characterization, phase transition tracking, and structural mode analysis in functional ceramics.

Vector Network Analyzer (VNA)

Microwave characterization of substrates and dielectric resonators up to 20 GHz for wireless communication and satellite applications.

Major Proposals & Initiatives

High-impact research proposals and technology development programmes

Nanotechnology

National Nanotechnology Initiative Proposal

A large-scale infrastructure proposal submitted to DST under the NNP programme for establishing a Centre of Excellence in Functional Ceramics and Oxide Electronics at IIT Guwahati, integrating synthesis, characterization, and device fabrication facilities.

Proposed Budget: ₹250 Lakhs · Agency: DST-NNP

Clean Energy

All-Solid-State Battery Technology Transfer

Technology development and transfer initiative for oxide solid electrolytes applicable in electric vehicles and grid storage. Collaborative proposal with industry partner for DSIR–CSIR–Industry joint programme.

Proposed Budget: ₹180 Lakhs · Agency: DST-DSIR

Space Technology

Advanced Microwave Substrate Development

Structural grant application to ISRO SAC for continued development of temperature-stable, low-loss ceramic substrates for high-frequency satellite communication modules and radar subsystems.

Proposed Budget: ₹75 Lakhs · Agency: ISRO/SAC

Biomedical

Piezoelectric Bioscaffolds for Neural Regeneration

Interdisciplinary proposal combining materials physics and biomedical engineering for developing piezo-active polymer-ceramic composite scaffolds for peripheral nerve regeneration, submitted under DBT-BioCARe.

Proposed Budget: ₹58 Lakhs · Agency: DBT
